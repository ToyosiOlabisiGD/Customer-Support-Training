Day 1 – QA & Calibration Foundations
  • 👩‍🏫 Recap – QA doc follow-up, calibration notes, and reflections
  • 🧠 QA Scorecard Refresh 2024 – (Reading, 30 mins)
  • 🧠 CSAT Overview – What is CSAT and how do we measure it? (30 mins)
  • 🧠 VOC Progress Reports – How we review feedback (30 mins)
  • 👩‍🏫 TL-led Calibration Discussion – Review real examples and align standards (30 mins)
Day 2 – Bug Handling Deep Dive
  • 🧠 Site Bug Process (Lessonly) – Full process walkthrough (90 mins)
  • 🧠 Bug Status Definitions – Learn each status label and their use (15 mins)
  • 🧠 C&C Admin Bug Process – Internal tracking & documentation flow (60 mins)
  • 👩‍🏫 TL-led QA Session – Bug classification examples + edge cases (45 mins)
Day 3 – Bug Practice + Review Tools
  • 🧠 Bug Newsletter (Looking Glass) – Review recent bugs to understand impact (15 mins)
  • 🧠 Help Center: Troubleshooting Tips + HackerOne Program (20 mins)
  • 🧠 Practice Cases – 3 Site Bug cases in Salesforce (60 mins)
  • 👩‍🏫 Shadowing – Observe bug case handling in real-time (30 mins)
Day 4 – QA + Bug Hybrid Day
  • 👩‍🏫 Recap – Questions from Bug Cases + QA clarifications
  • 🧠 Sensitive Case Types – General (Revisit via Guru card) (30 mins)
  • 🧠 Help Center: GDPR / CCPA / Privacy Resources (30 mins)
  • 👩‍🏫 Peer QA Session – Practice evaluating sample cases (60 mins)
  • 🧠 Guru: Groundrules + Escalation Criteria Review (30 mins)

  • ✅ Shadow Live Queue – Specialty area bug cases (120 mins)
  • ✅ Final Practice Case: General + Bug Scenario (60 mins)
  • 👩‍🏫 TL Debrief – Final QA sync + targeted coaching (30 mins)

Week 5: Personalization & Final Wrap
Day 1 – Tools Personalization & Workflow Refresh
  • 👩‍🏫 Recap – Reflections and QA sync with TL
  • 🧠 Salesforce: Add photo, notification settings, “About Me” section, OOO setup (60 mins)
  • 🧠 Email & Calendar: Set timezone, create signature, add weekly reminders (30 mins)
  • 🧠 Guru: Follow Core Collections, pin most-used cards (30 mins)
  • 🧠 Lessonly: Review “Learn” tab for refresher or remaining modules (30 mins)
Day 2 – Shadowing & Feedback Practice
  • 👩‍🏫 Recap – Daily kickoff + open Q&A with TL
  • ✅ Live Queue: Shadow Specialty Area (120 mins)
  • 🧠 Peer Practice: Shadow and give QA-style feedback on 1 case (30 mins)
  • 👩‍🏫 Feedback Review: TL or QA walks through sample case and scoring rubric (30 mins)
Day 3 – Writing Workshop II
  • 🧠 Writing Workshop II – Personalization in Support Emails (Watch + Deck + 1 Case Practice) (180 mins)
  • 🧠 Offshore Writing Workshop 2024 – Review deck and personalize response examples
  • 🧠 Practice Case: Follow scenario from deck and submit a response for review (30 mins)
Day 4 – Full Queue Readiness
  • 👩‍🏫 Recap – Final check-in + live queue goals
  • ✅ Live Queue: Jobseeker/Employer FAQs, Bugs, Product Support (120 mins)
  • ✅ Peer QA Feedback: Review each other’s case responses and reflect on trends (30 mins)
  • 🧠 TL Wrap-Up: 1:1s or group sync to go over any open gaps or tools (30 mins)
Day 5 – Final Review & Next Steps
  • ✅ Live Queue: Solo work – Handle real cases across categories (120 mins)
  • 🧠 Self-Assessment: Complete end-of-training reflections or checklist (30 mins)
  • 👩‍🏫 Final 1:1 or Group Feedback Session with TL (30 mins)
  • ✅ Wrap-Up – Celebrate training completion and discuss next phase!
