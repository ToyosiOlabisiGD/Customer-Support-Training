Employer Support Training

6-Week Onboarding Program

Week 1: Foundations & Compliance (Employer Path)

Day 1: Welcome, Mission & Tool Access
  • Begin C&C Security + Privacy modules (Lessonly)
Day 2: Salesforce Navigation + Case Types
  • Review: Account Management, Content Issues, Flagged Reviews
Day 3: Compliance Overview & T&S Escalations
  • Group discussion: common privacy and review risks
Day 4: Introduction to Employer Tools
  • Walkthrough: responding to flagged reviews
Day 5: Wrap-Up & Review
  • 1:1 with TL to discuss progress + Week 2 preview

Week 2: Core Case Types & Tools

Day 1: Review Management Cases
  • Complete Review Escalation (Lessonly)
Day 2: Account Access + Login Issues
  • Hands-on practice using reset tools
Day 3: QA Calibration
  • Practice QA on 3 sample cases
Day 4: Handling Multi-Team Escalations
  • Internal comms: notes, handoffs, and tags
Day 5: Form Review & Tools Mastery
  • Knowledge quiz + feedback session

Week 3: Intermediate Case Work & QA Focus

Day 1: Queue Ownership Practice
  • Live QA session on tone & accuracy
Day 2: Deep Dive - Flagged Reviews
  • Shadow + solo mock cases
Day 3: Bug Reporting
  • Complete Site Bug Process (Lessonly)
Day 4: Peer QA Roundtable
  • Discussion: tricky case handling
Day 5: Self-Eval & Progress Planning
  • TL sync for Week 4 readiness

Week 4: Ramp-Up Support

Day 1: Live Queue Supervision
  • Flag escalation-worthy interactions
Day 2: Cross-Queue Exposure
  • Identify Employer-Jobseeker overlap
Day 3: Shadow to Solo Practice
  • Track metrics and report blockers
Day 4: QA Deep-Dive
  • Revisit missed cases with coach
Day 5: End-of-Week Challenge
  • Submit peer QA on another trainee

Week 5: Independent Queue Work

Day 1: 80% Productivity Target
  • Submit 2 self-QAs
Day 2: Content Accuracy + Tag Mastery
  • Guru + tracker spot checks
Day 3: Partner Coaching
  • Get written feedback on 2 cases
Day 4: Communication Focus
  • Coach review on empathy and escalation phrasing
Day 5: TL Check-In & Prep
  • Final adjustment to QA style

Week 6: Full Production

Day 1: Independent Work + Metrics
  • Track personal case stats
Day 2: QA Challenge
  • Live QA defense with TL
Day 3: Ready to Ramp
  • Check off Readiness Checklist
Day 4: Graduation Prep
  • Optional peer kudos + reflections
