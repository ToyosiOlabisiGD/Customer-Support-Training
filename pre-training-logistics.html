Pre-Training Logistics Checklist

Glassdoor Trust & Safety Offshore Onboarding

Use this checklist before Week 1 begins

Day 0 – Setup & Access
  • 👩‍🏫 Meet the TL & Team Captain (20 mins)
  • 🧠 Complete Vendor Bio Book (15 mins)
  • 🧠 VPN Setup – Confirm access (🇧🇬 Bulgarian trainees)
  • 🧠 Photo ID + Access Card Setup (🇨🇴 Colombian trainees)
  • 🧠 Tool Access Test – Okta, Salesforce, Jira, Lessonly, TICK, Zoom/GMeet, Email, Guru, Aquarium, Looking Glass, etc. (180 mins)

Day 0 – Compliance & Orientation
  • 🧠 Attend NHO Sessions – HR, Payroll, Security, Facilities (🇨🇴 🇧🇬)
  • 🧠 Complete SAP Courses (if assigned)
  • 🧠 Complete Anonymity & Privacy Policy (Lessonly – 60 mins)
  • 🧠 Review Violent Acts / Self-Harm Escalation Protocol (40 mins)
  • 🧠 Review Glassdoor Code of Ethics, Care Downtime Policy, Partner Content Policy
