Jobseeker Support Training

6-Week Onboarding Program

Week 1: Foundations & Compliance

Day 1: Onboarding, Mission & Tools
  • Meet the TL and team
  • Review company mission, vision, and values
  • Complete C&C Security, Privacy Escalation modules (Lessonly)
  • Set up required accounts and workspace tools (Gmail, Slack, Salesforce)
Day 2: Salesforce Navigation
  • Instructor-led Salesforce walkthrough
  • Complete Salesforce Basics module (Lessonly)
  • Practice creating and resolving test cases
Day 3: Queue Processes & Escalation Overview
  • Review jobseeker workflow overview doc
  • Complete Escalation Policy & Floor Support (Lessonly)
  • Shadow queue triage process
Day 4: Legal & Compliance Readiness
  • Complete Legal Requests, Anonymous/Private Info modules (Lessonly)
  • Review Legal Request Tracker & T&S escalation flow
  • QA walkthrough of sample compliance case
Day 5: Systems Integration & Weekly Recap
  • Intro to Google Sheets Tracker, Guru Cards, and Feedback Forms
  • Weekly knowledge check quiz
  • Group discussion on Week 1 learnings

Week 2: Core Tools & Flagged Content

Day 1: FAQ Modules + Account Support
  • Complete Care FAQs, Account Maintenance (Lessonly)
  • Begin observing account support tickets (shadow)
Day 2: Profile Management + Reinstatement
  • Instructor-led session on profile issues
  • Review Guru Cards for Reinstatement
Day 3: Flagged Content & Policy Review
  • Complete Flagged Content Training (Lessonly)
  • Work through 3 sample cases
Day 4: Tool Practice + Escalations
  • Hands-on review of flagged content tools
  • Partner QA calibration session
Day 5: Weekly Wrap-Up
  • Knowledge check quiz
  • TL Q&A session and feedback roundtable

Week 3: Core Support & Practice

Day 1: Content Issues & Bug Reporting
  • Complete Content Issues, Site Bug Process modules
  • Admin Bug Process guide review
Day 2: Jobseeker FAQs & Shadowing
  • Complete Jobseeker FAQ and Product Support modules
  • Observe real jobseeker case handling
Day 3: Troubleshooting Deep Dive
  • Instructor-led session: Common Troubleshooting Patterns
  • Practice 5 troubleshooting simulations
Day 4: Independent Queue Time + QA Review
  • Handle test cases solo (~3 hours)
  • QA partner debrief
Day 5: Feedback & Readiness Check
  • Run readiness self-assessment
  • One-on-one with TL to review progress

Week 4: Ramp-Up Phase

Day 1: Start Live Case Handling (Supervised)
  • Begin handling live jobseeker tickets (~3 hours)
  • Daily QA check-ins
Day 2: Escalation Decision-Making
  • Instructor session: escalation vs. solve
  • Work through 5 triage decision scenarios
Day 3: Queue Management Tools
  • Tool practice: assigning, tagging, tracking cases
  • Review KPIs: first response time, resolution time
Day 4: Midweek Performance Review
  • Check-in with TL on performance trends
  • Self-review of 3 handled cases
Day 5: Full Day in Queue (Supervised)
  • Target: 5–6 hours of queue time
  • Optional peer feedback form

Week 5: Increased Autonomy

Day 1: Expanded Queue Work
  • Handle broader case types independently
  • Daily QA check-ins
Day 2: Advanced Troubleshooting
  • Instructor-led session on uncommon case types
  • Shadow and replicate resolution patterns
Day 3: Productivity vs. Quality
  • Data review of speed vs. accuracy metrics
  • Practice with QA scoring rubrics
Day 4: Case Ownership & Documentation
  • Deep dive: effective internal notes
  • Practice updating escalation trackers
Day 5: Mid-Ramp Feedback
  • TL-led case review
  • Identify growth areas for final week

Week 6: Full Production

Day 1: Independent Queue Day
  • Target: 6–7 hours live handling
  • Log end-of-day reflections
Day 2: Deep QA Calibration
  • Compare QA scores with peers
  • Refine approach to case tone and user trust
Day 3: Independent Queue Day
  • Focus on accuracy and documentation
  • Start readiness checklist
Day 4: Final Queue Push
  • Target 8 hours of consistent support
  • QA 2 peer cases for cross-feedback
Day 5: Graduation Day
  • TL-led 1:1 debrief + certification
  • Wrap-up survey + team welcome
